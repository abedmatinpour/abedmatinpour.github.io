Research Assistant

Centre of Excellence for Research in Adaptive Systems
PI: Prof. Marin Litoiu
York University, Toronto, Ontario
February 2025 – Present

Leading advanced research in Large Language Models (LLMs), adaptive systems, and performance optimization. Focus on developing scalable AI systems for real-world applications with emphasis on efficiency and accuracy.

Key Research Contributions

  • Large Language Model Optimization: Fine-tuned language models from 8B to 700B MoE architectures using LoRA-based parameter-efficient tuning and supervised feedback loops on domain-specific and math reasoning datasets, increasing accuracy while minimizing compute and storage overhead.
  • Adaptive Context Windowing: Experimented with adaptive context windowing strategies using similarity-based ranking, boosting math reasoning performance on smaller models with reduced context length and latency.
  • RAG Pipeline Development: Built scalable retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines using LangChain and LlamaIndex, enabling low-latency, context-aware QA and summarization across distributed and hybrid deployments.
  • Performance Optimization: Leveraged TensorRT-LLM to optimize inference performance for large models and achieving significant latency and throughput improvements for real-time applications.
  • Automated Testing Framework: Developed an automated, adaptive stress and load testing framework powered by LLM-driven agents, cutting test iteration counts by over 40% compared to baseline load-testing approaches.
  • Benchmarking Framework: Designed a comprehensive benchmarking framework for measuring accuracy, latency, GPU utilization, and energy efficiency across edge, cloud, and hybrid environments.

Publications in Progress

  • Two publications (on benchmarking and adaptive LLM-agent testing) in progress for submission to FSE as First Author

Key Research Contributions

  • Advanced Medical Image Segmentation: Applied U-Net++, DeepLabV3+, and FPN for tumor detection on mouse brain MRI datasets, improving segmentation accuracy through channel optimization and output fusion.
  • Optimization Algorithm Integration: Integrated Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO), Dynamic Whale Optimization Algorithm (DWOA), and fuzzy logic-driven methods for channel selection and weight tuning, enhancing clinical imaging pipelines.
  • Game Theory Applications: Introduced game theory-based output concatenation techniques, increasing prediction accuracy across multi-model tumor segmentation workflows.
  • Speech Processing Research: Fine-tuned DistilHuBERT for stuttering detection and utilized Whisper for word-by-word speech transcription, achieving improved detection precision and fluency assessment.
  • Materials Science Applications: Developed a bi-directional GAN for crystal bandgap prediction, advancing machine learning applications in materials science.
  • Wearable Health Technology: Designed a wearable device for heart attack prediction by converting PCG signals to ECG-like signals using dual ML models; contributed to both software and embedded hardware development.

Research Publications

  • Dataset Development: Collected and annotated custom datasets across varied conditions (low light, occlusions, and heavy traffic) to improve model robustness and accuracy for real-world deployment.
