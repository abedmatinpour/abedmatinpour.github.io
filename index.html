About Me

I am a Machine Learning researcher and AI expert currently pursuing my Master of Science at York University, Toronto. My research spans multiple cutting-edge domains including Large Language Models (LLMs), Generative AI, Computer Vision, Multimodal Systems, and Causal Inference.

With hands-on experience in developing scalable retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines, optimizing deep learning models using TensorRT-LLM, and creating automated testing frameworks, I bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical applications in machine learning.

I am actively involved in research at three different laboratories at York University, contributing to publications in top-tier conferences and developing innovative solutions in generative AI, multimodal systems, and causal inference.
