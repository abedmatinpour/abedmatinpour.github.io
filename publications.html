Optimized DeepLabV3+ for Clinical Data Analysis through Advanced Particle Swarm Optimization-Based Channel Selection

Breast Cancer Segmentation Using a Modified U-Net with Dynamic Whale Optimization Algorithm (DWOA) Channel Selection

MVML 2025 Paris, France August 2025 Accepted
Authors: Norouziazad, A., Matinpour, A., Deljoo, F., Homam, B., Trivedi, B., & Salahandish, R.

Abstract

This research introduces a novel approach to breast cancer segmentation utilizing a modified U-Net architecture enhanced with Dynamic Whale Optimization Algorithm (DWOA) for intelligent channel selection. The methodology demonstrates superior performance in medical image segmentation tasks.
